Sir Vivian Fuchs
1908 - 1999

Welcome to the website of Sir Vivian Fuchs. This site tells the story of his life and work, and commemorates his achievement as leader of the Commonwealth Trans-Antarctic Expedition on the first land crossing of the Antarctic in 1958.

Illustrated with photographs from his family's collection, this site is a unique memorial to one of history's great explorers. Many of the images in the collection are for sale, and digital versions will soon be available for purchase (royalty-free) via this website. Please contact Fuchs Archives Ltd. to find out more.
